Yoga Flow Workout

Warm-Up (5 min)

  • Easy seated stretches
  • Neck rolls
  • Shoulder rolls

Yoga Flow Sequence

  • Cat-Cow Stretch - 5 breaths
  • Downward Dog - 5 breaths
  • Warrior I (Right & Left) - 5 breaths each side
  • Warrior II (Right & Left) - 5 breaths each side
  • Triangle Pose (Right & Left) - 5 breaths each side
  • Tree Pose (Right & Left) - hold 20–30 seconds each
  • Seated Forward Fold - 5 breaths
  • Bridge Pose - 5–10 breaths
  • Supine Twist (Right & Left) - 5 breaths each side

Cool Down (3–5 min)

  • Happy Baby Pose
  • Legs up the wall (optional)
  • Savasana - 2–5 minutes of deep breathing

Tips

  • Move with your breath.
  • Focus on gentle stretches, don’t push beyond comfort.
  • Relax and enjoy the flow.
