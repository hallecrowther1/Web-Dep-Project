Pilates Workout

Warm-Up (5 min)

  • Deep breathing while lying on your back
  • Shoulder rolls
  • Pelvic tilts

Pilates Exercises

  • The Hundred - 3 sets of 10–15 breaths
  • Roll-Up - 3 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Single-Leg Stretch - 3 sets of 10 reps each leg
  • Double-Leg Stretch - 3 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Leg Circles - 3 sets of 5 circles each leg (both directions)
  • Rolling Like a Ball - 3 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Plank with Leg Lift - 3 sets of 10 reps each leg
  • Side-Lying Leg Lifts - 3 sets of 10–12 reps each leg
  • Swimming - 3 sets of 20–30 seconds

Cool Down (3–5 min)

  • Spinal twist stretch
  • Child's pose
  • Deep breathing and relaxation

Tips

  • Move with control, don't rush.
  • Focus on your core and posture.
  • Breathe deeply and consistently throughout.
