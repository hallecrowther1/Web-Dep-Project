Upper Arm Day Workout

Warm-Up (5–10 min)

  • Arm circles
  • Light dumbbell curls
  • Tricep stretches

Biceps

  • Dumbbell bicep curls - 3 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Hammer curls - 3 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Concentration curls - 3 sets of 8–10 reps each arm

Triceps

  • Tricep dips - 3 sets of 10–15 reps
  • Overhead tricep extensions - 3 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns - 3 sets of 10–12 reps

Optional Finisher

10 bicep curls + 10 tricep dips, repeat 3 times with little rest.

Lower Leg Day Workout

Warm-Up (5–10 min)

  • Light walking or jogging
  • Ankle circles
  • Bodyweight calf raises

Calves

  • Standing calf raises - 3 sets of 12–15 reps
  • Seated calf raises - 3 sets of 12–15 reps
  • Single-leg calf raises - 3 sets of 10–12 reps each leg

Shins (Front of Lower Leg)

  • Toe raises (leaning against wall or seated) - 3 sets of 12–15 reps
  • Resistance band dorsiflexion - 3 sets of 12–15 reps each foot

Optional Finisher

15 calf raises + 15 toe raises, repeat 3 times with little rest.

Tips

  • Move slowly and control the stretch and squeeze.
  • Pause at the top of each rep for better activation.
  • Rest 30–60 seconds between sets.
